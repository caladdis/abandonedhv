Welcome

Welcome to the underbelly of the Lower Hudson Valley where history meets mystery. Discover the haunting allure of the Lower Hudson Valley's abandoned structures. From forgotten mansions to deserted factories, each abandoned structure carries whispers of its former glory, waiting to be rediscovered and appreciated. explore the map to find out more.

About

The Lower Hudson Valley, steeped in rich history and surrounded by breathtaking landscapes, holds a hidden treasure trove of forgotten architecture. Through stunning imagery, gripping narratives, and meticulous research, WE AIM to breathe new life into these neglected spaces. Our mission is to shed light on the beauty and melancholy of these abandoned places, revealing the intricate layers of their history and the impact they once had on the local communities.
